Beer Bread

Description

Simple quick bread requiring no leaven, rising, or kneading - only a bottle of beer.

Source: The Savage & The Sage

Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups of flour
  • 11.5 ounces beer
  • 1 tablespoon salt (+ a pinch for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Add all dry ingredients to large mixing bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center. Add beer and 1 tablespoon melted butter.
  3. Mix all ingredients together by hand starting from center and stirring outwards in concentric circles. The dough should quickly come together to form a shaggy mass in 3 or 4 turns.
  4. Shape the dough into a round and transfer to 8-inch skillet.
  5. Bake in oven for 20-25 minutes, or until nicely browned. Lower temperature to 325°F and bake another 25 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven. Brush with remaining tablespoon of butter and a pinch of salt.

Notes

  • The flavor profile of this bread will be largely determined by the beer used.
  • In place of beer, it is possible to use hard cider or a carbonated beverage.
